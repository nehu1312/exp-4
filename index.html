Spaghetti Carbonara

Preparation Time

  • Preparation: 15 minutes
  • Cooking: 15 minutes
  • Total: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces (340 grams) spaghetti
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup (100 grams) freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 ounces (115 grams) pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta cooking water, then drain the pasta.
  2. Whisk together the egg yolks, egg, and Parmesan cheese in a large bowl.
  3. Cook the pancetta or bacon in a large skillet over medium heat until crisp. Add the minced garlic and saut√© for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Add the drained spaghetti to the skillet and mix well with the pancetta/bacon.
  5. Remove the skillet from heat and add the egg mixture to the spaghetti, stirring quickly to create a creamy sauce. Adjust with pasta cooking water if necessary.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, and garnish with fresh parsley if desired. Serve immediately.
